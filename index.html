Alejandro Oporto Velásquez

PhD Candidate in Economics · University of Girona

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the University of Girona, supervised by Dr. Andreas Kyriacou. My research focuses on applied econometrics, fiscal decentralization, and public policy evaluation. Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant at the Institut d'Economia de Barcelona (IEB) and the University of Barcelona. I hold a Master's degree in Economics from the University of Barcelona and the University of Copenhagen.
