Alejandro Oporto Velásquez

PhD candidate in Economics at the University of Girona, under the supervision of Dr. Andreas Kyriacou. His research focuses on applied econometrics, fiscal decentralization, and public policy evaluation. He previously worked as a Research Assistant at the Institut d’Economia de Barcelona (IEB) and the University of Barcelona. He holds a Master’s degree in Economics from the University of Barcelona and the University of Copenhagen.
